More Than Votes

What does this do?
 Put in a zip code, and it will (hopefully) take you to a page for your local municipality's meetings, be they city council, county commissioners, etc.

How does this work?
 This does a reverse lookup on your zip code, finds your county or city, and performs a Google search for some terms to direct you to the right page.

So it's just a google search?
 Yes, but sometimes people need a little push.

Why did you do this?
 No matter where you sit politically, we should all be more civically minded in our communities. Especially if you don't like where things are today.
